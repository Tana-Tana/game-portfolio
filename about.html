About Us

Hello! We are Promici Studio, a passionate Game Development team. Our members proudly come from the PTIT Programming Club and PTIT Multimedia Club, which belong to the S2B Student Talent Development Community.

With our combined experience in Unity and participation in multiple Game Jams, we love tackling technical challenges and polishing "game feel" to deliver the best gameplay possible.

Our Vision & Mission: We are dedicated to contributing to the growth of the Vietnamese gaming market and strive to put the Vietnam game industry on the global map.

Our Achievements

1st Place - Global Game Jam 2025 (Vietnam)

Won the digital game category with our puzzle platformer "Bubble Tea Go", created entirely within 36 hours.

Top Participants - Voodoo Game Jam 2026

Developed "Grind Shot" under the guidance of Voodoo mentors, focusing on hyper-casual mechanics. We are proud to be one of the 24 teams in Vietnam selected to participate in the Voodoo Game Jam.
